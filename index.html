About The Event

Explorica is one of the signature events conducted by IEEE SB LBSITW. This time again we are back with an array of exciting events. IEEE Student Branch of LBS Institute of Technology for Women takes much pride and fervour in hosting this three-day event,"EXPLORICA 3.0 - Crafted to Unite Technology and Fine Earth", with the theme "Climate action, Clean and Affordable Energy".

Where

LBS Institute of Technology for Women, Poojappura, Trivandrum

When

3rd, 4th, and 5th December
Friday to Sunday

This time, we are conducting Explorica with a social cause,

"Disaster Resilience in Kerala"
